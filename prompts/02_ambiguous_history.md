# 02 Ambiguous History

Slide title for later PPT overlay:
貢獻巨大，但並非潔白無瑕

Core message for later PPT overlay:
蘇聯確實承受巨大犧牲並擊敗納粹，但這不等於它的整段戰時歷史都是單純正義。

Slide design prompt:
Create a 16:9 explanatory slide with a balanced "two truths" structure. Use a clean split composition: left side shows dark archival fragments suggesting the prewar pact, spheres of influence, border maps, and refugee columns; right side shows the Eastern Front scale through distant battlefield horizon, ruined winter landscape, and mass sacrifice symbolism. Between the two sides, place a neutral central weighing-scale or balance motif made of light, map lines, and document edges, without literal labels. Reserve the top band for a Traditional Chinese title and the bottom third for three concise bullet blocks. Use aged paper textures, charcoal shadows, subdued crimson, cold blue-gray smoke, and restrained documentary collage. The slide must be readable as an argument structure, not just atmosphere. Do not render readable text.

Text overlay plan:
- Title: 貢獻巨大，但並非潔白無瑕
- Thesis: 歷史判斷需要同時看見「反納粹犧牲」與「早期權力交易」。
- Bullets:

Design notes:
- The visual should help the speaker explain nuance, not push a single heroic image.
- Avoid glorification.
- No readable text, no swastikas, no hammer-and-sickle close-ups, no real person likenesses, no gore.
