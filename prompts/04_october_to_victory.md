# 04 October To Victory

Slide title for later PPT overlay:
從十月革命閱兵到勝利日閱兵

Core message for later PPT overlay:
蘇聯時代的核心儀式曾指向革命與未來；俄羅斯時代則越來越轉向二戰勝利與過去。

Slide design prompt:
Create a 16:9 explanatory slide centered on a horizontal timeline. Use two large visual panels: left panel suggests Cold War-era Soviet October Revolution parade through abstract missile silhouettes, armored columns, formation geometry, and red ceremonial light; right panel suggests post-Soviet Victory Day memory through an older tank, veteran-memory symbolism, security barriers, and a colder emptier square. Connect the panels with a central timeline arrow and a visible transition zone made of fading archival film grain. Reserve the top for a Traditional Chinese title and reserve the lower band for a short comparison table with two columns. Make the design presentation-first: clear panel division, simple shapes, readable negative space, restrained historical imagery. Do not render readable text or exact symbols.

Text overlay plan:
- Title: 從十月革命閱兵到勝利日閱兵
- Thesis: 國家敘事的重心，從「未來的革命理想」轉向「過去的勝利記憶」。
- Comparison:
  - 蘇聯時期：革命、工業化、未來烏托邦
  - 俄羅斯時期：二戰勝利、苦難記憶、民族凝聚
  - 變化含義：合法性來源發生轉移

Design notes:
- Use as the clearest structural transition slide in the deck.
- Avoid exact flags or readable insignia; keep symbols abstract.
- No readable text, no exact flags, no real leaders, no logos.
