# 06 Depleting Legacy

Slide title for later PPT overlay:
烏克蘭戰爭正在透支二戰遺產

Core message for later PPT overlay:
當現實戰爭與「反侵略、反法西斯」的歷史形象衝突，二戰遺產會被快速消耗。

Slide design prompt:
Create a 16:9 explanatory slide with a "moral capital balance sheet" visual metaphor. Left side: a small stack of tarnished medals, archival photos, and parade symbols labeled only by blank graphic blocks, representing inherited WWII legitimacy. Right side: distant damaged urban silhouettes, smoke, cracked pavement, and military tracks, implied without explicit combat or bodies. In the center, show a simple downward gauge, draining reservoir, or balance-scale motif to communicate depletion. Reserve the top left for a Traditional Chinese title and thesis; reserve the bottom band for three short cause-effect bullets. Use slate gray, tarnished gold, dark red accents, cold daylight, realistic stone texture, and sober analytical mood. Do not render readable text.

Text overlay plan:
- Title: 烏克蘭戰爭正在透支二戰遺產
- Thesis: 越把當下戰爭包裝成歷史正義，越會消耗原有的道德資本。
- Bullets:
  - 二戰記憶：反侵略、反納粹、巨大犧牲
  - 現實衝突：主權、侵略、國際法爭議
  - 結果：勝利敘事越用越薄

Design notes:
- Make the depletion logic visually explicit.
- Avoid explicit combat, corpses, gore, or identifiable soldiers.
- No readable text, no flags with clear insignia, no real people, no corpses, no gore.
