Core message for later PPT overlay:
日期差異不只是時差問題，也關乎「誰來定義戰爭結束」。

Slide design prompt:
Create a 16:9 explanatory slide layout with a clear two-column comparison. Left column: a simple editorial timeline diagram with two large blank calendar cards, one associated with Western Europe and one with Moscow time, connected by a thin time-zone line over a ghosted map of Europe. Right column: a reserved text panel with space for a Traditional Chinese title, one-sentence thesis, and three short bullets. In the background, use a dim archival room with a faint surrender-table silhouette, papers, lamps, and map fragments, but no identifiable real people. The slide should feel like a history explainer, not a painting: structured, legible, and easy to present. Use muted archival beige, gray, dark red accents, and low-contrast cinematic lighting. Do not render readable text or numbers in the image.
