# 05 Memory As Legitimacy

Slide title for later PPT overlay:
當未來破滅，過去成為凝聚力

Core message for later PPT overlay:
當新的未來願景不足以凝聚社會，國家更依賴共同苦難與勝利記憶。

Slide design prompt:
Create a 16:9 explanatory slide using a central "legitimacy engine" diagram. In the center, show a glowing archive-like core made of wartime photographs, medals, candles, and document fragments, treated as a symbolic power source. Around it, use three clean outer nodes represented by silhouettes or abstract icons: citizens, soldiers, and officials, all connected back to the archive by thin light lines. Reserve the left side for a Traditional Chinese title and thesis; reserve the lower right for a small three-point explanation block. The environment is a dim civic hall, cold and economically worn, but the layout must remain clean and instructional. Use restrained red-gold light from the archive, desaturated gray surroundings, subtle dust, and simple diagram geometry. Do not render readable text.

Text overlay plan:
- Title: 當未來破滅，過去成為凝聚力
- Thesis: 勝利記憶成為替代未來願景的政治能源。
- Bullets:
  - 共同苦難：提供情感連結
  - 歷史榮光：提供道德正當性
  - 外部敵人：提供重新動員的理由

Design notes:
- Keep it explanatory; avoid making the archive look too religious or mystical.
- The central metaphor should be understandable in one glance.
- No readable text, no specific flags, no real people, no gore.
